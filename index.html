About Us

Abi and Courtney met in England in early 2016 when Courtney worked at a school in London. They quickly discovered a shared love of spending time outdoors, and even went on their first backpacking trip together as a third date! Since moving to California in the fall of 2017, they have had ample opportunities to enjoy roadtrips in the western US, hiking in the Sierras, and even scuba diving in Hawaii. They have also learned to mountain bike (slowly) and surf (pretty badly). In June of 2020, they got married in a local courthouse in California. They are so excited to finally celebrate their wedding with you all!

How to get to Cambridge and Accommodation

The wedding will be at Westminster College, Madingley Road, Cambridge, UK. CB3 0AA. Cambridge is 1 hr from London on the train. Parking in the town can be difficult and expensive, so we recommend that overseas guests avoid bringing rental cars.

We recommend staying within Cambridge city center so that everything is within walking distance.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Cambridge Botanical Garden
  • Take a guided or self-guided historical walking tour
  • Visit King's College chapel
  • Go punt yourself!
  • Enjoy brunch at Fitzbillies
  • Visit the Corpus Christi clock
  • Explore the Market
  • Climb the tower for a great view from St. Mary's Church
  • Grab an underground cocktail at Hidden Rooms
  • Enjoy a cocktail on the rooftop of the Varsity Hotel
  • Visit one of the Cambridge University museums
  • Rent a bike to experience the town like a student
  • Check out the new bouldering gym
  • Take a refreshing swim at the Jesus Green Lido
