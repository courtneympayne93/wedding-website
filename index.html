About Us

Abi and Courtney met in England in early 2016 when Courtney worked at a school in London. They quickly discovered a shared love of spending time outdoors, and even went on their first backpacking trip together as a third date! Since moving to California in the fall of 2017, they have had ample opportunities to enjoy roadtrips in the western US, hiking in the Sierras, and even scuba diving in Hawaii. They have also learned to mountain bike (slowly) and surf (pretty badly). In June of 2020, they got married in a local courthouse in California. They are so excited to finally celebrate their wedding with you all!

How to get to Cambridge and Accommodation

The wedding will be at Westminster College, Madingley Road, Cambridge, UK. CB3 0AA. While there will be some limited parking at Westminster on the day of the wedding, parking in the town can be difficult and expensive. Since Cambridge is only one hour from London, we recommend that overseas guests take the train.

We recommend staying within Cambridge city center so that everything is within walking distance.

Things to Do

If you have some spare time while in Cambridge, we'd recommend visiting one of the Cambridge University museums or enjoying a guided or self-guided historical walking tour of Cambridge. You could even rent a bike to experience the town like a student! More interested in viewing classic college architecture? Check out the King's College chapel or the Corpus Christi clock. You could even climb the tower for a great view of the town from St. Mary's Church. If you'd prefer to spend time outdoors, you might enjoy a trip to the Cambridge Botanical Garden, giving punting a go, swimming at the Jesus Green Lido, or an exploration of the Market. Finally, refresh from all your exploration with brunch at Fitzbillies, an underground cocktail at Hidden Rooms, or a cocktail on the rooftop of the Varsity Hotel.
